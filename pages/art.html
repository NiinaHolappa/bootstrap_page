How I started

I was never interested or good at art as a child. I haven't been talented at it from young age as many other people who are good at drawing or painting. I started to get interested in it about two years ago when I started to watch Bob Ross's Youtube videos. He made painting look easy and fun so I decided to give it a go.

More

Painting is more like a hobby for me rather than something I would take more serious interest in. I like to paint sometimes just for fun. Nowadays I don't have much time to paint so it's been a while since my latest painting. I have painted a few paintings for my friends and if someone was interested to purchase my paintings, I would be happy to sell them.
